Introducing Ernest Moses

Ernest Moses is a Consultant, Author, Minister of the Word and a Youth & Leadership Coach. He is an author of many books and models of human behaviour including ‘Young Achievers’. Ernest is also a transformational speaker as he enables his audiences to be change makers in their given industries. Ernest has a mandate for raising model leaders to be change makers. He achieves this by enabling every individual to discover their inner self and live to the greatest heights of their achievement. His primary message is teaching individuals the principle of Living from the Inside Out. He is the founder and director of Young Achievers Group – a community of young achievers who believe in making a difference in their world by discovering their inborn gifts and talents and contributing meaningfully to their society

Training Expertise

Ernest Moses has been training and coaching youths and young adults for over ten (10) years. He studied Bsc Psychology and also holds a Youth Work Practice certificate from London Youth. He has served as a youth leader at Kings temple (ICGC) for over a decade helping the youths to discover their purpose and shape their vision in life

Author

He is the author of a youth focused book titled Young Achievers and also delivers several online inspirational programmes among them are living from the inside out, Young Achiever Model Series, and many more. Ernest also runs a leadership and coaching company, where he has developed models to help young people discover their purpose and stay focus in life.

Transformational Speaker

Ernest agrees with the saying that “the value of a person is not in what he or she has but in the quality of his personality” therefore he aims at adding value to the lives of every young person he comes into contact with. He strongly believes that every young person has a potential that when unleash will enable him or her to contribute greatly to their society.
